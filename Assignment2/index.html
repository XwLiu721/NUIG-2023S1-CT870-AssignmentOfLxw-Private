About Us

Unity Technologies is a video game development company best known as the creator of Unity, a cross-platform game engine used to develop video games for PC, consoles, mobile devices and websites. The company was founded in Denmark in 2004 and is headquartered in San Francisco, California. Unity is used to develop popular titles across many genres, including AAA games, indies, augmented and virtual reality content. Some of the most well-known games developed with Unity include Pokemon GO, Hellblade: Senua's Sacrifice, Cuphead and Angry Birds. In addition to the game engine software, Unity Technologies offers other tools, services and solutions for game development. This includes Unity Ads for in-game monetization, Unity Analytics for analyzing player behavior, and Unity Collaborate for team-based development. Unity claims that over 50% of all new mobile games and nearly half of the Top 1000 mobile games worldwide have been built with Unity. It has become one of the most widely used game engines due to its capabilities, continuous updates and flexible business models. Unity Technologies' mission is to democratize game development and bring game development tools to all developers.
